How To Make

  • Line a 20 cm springform. Mix crushed biscuits with melted butter
    press into base. Chill 15 min.
  • gelatin in cold water (or prep agar per pack).
  • Whip cold cream to soft peaks; set aside.
  • Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  • Mix in mango purée.
  • Melt gelatin (barely warm) or simmer agar to activate whisk into filling.
  • Fold in whipped cream. Pour over crust. Chill 4–6 hrs.
  • Topping: Warm mango purée (+ a little sugar if needed). Stir in a
    little melted gelatin or activated agar. Cool 5 min.
  • Pour over set cake; chill 1–2 hrs. Unmold, garnish, serve.
